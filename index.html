Why Turbulence Matters 为什么湍流很重要

From the cream swirling in your coffee to the massive atmospheric currents shaping our weather, turbulence is everywhere. Yet it's one of the last great unsolved problems in classical physics. Let's explore why this chaotic phenomenon follows surprisingly elegant mathematical patterns. 从你咖啡中旋转的奶泡到塑造我们天气的巨大大气环流，湍流无处不在。 然而，它却是经典物理学中最后几个未解决的重大问题之一。 让我们探索为什么这种混沌现象遵循着出奇优雅的数学模式。

🧮 The Physics Behind It 🧮 背后的物理学

The Navier-Stokes equations describe fluid motion, but at high Reynolds numbers (Re = ρUL/μ), inertial forces dominate viscous forces, creating chaotic eddies across multiple scales—from millimeters to kilometers. 纳维-斯托克斯方程描述了流体运动，但在高雷诺数下 (Re = ρUL/μ)，惯性力主导粘性力，产生跨越多个尺度的混沌涡旋 ——从毫米到公里。
